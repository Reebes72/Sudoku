Scoring is based off of time taken to complete the puzzle, the number of hints you have used (if applicable), the use of puzzle assist, and number of correct answers.

Time Bonus: 	5000 points

Easy 
Full Bonus: 	5 Minutes
Half Bonus: 	10 Minutes

Medium
Full Bonus: 	10 Minutes
Half Bonus 	20 Minutes

Hard
Full Bonus: 	15 Minutes
Half Bonus: 	30 Minutes

Hints
On a successful completion of the puzzle, if you have used no hints, you will receive a 5000 point bonus.

Puzzle Assist
Using puzzle assist will dock your score by 1000 points.
